About

A helping hand, built on trust.

Handy Lady LLC is a one-woman handyperson business serving St. Petersburg, FL and the surrounding area. The goal is simple: show up, do good work, and charge a fair, predictable rate — no estimates, no upsells, no surprises on the bill.

Handy Lady LLC puts extra care into empowering and supporting female, LGBTQ+, neurodivergent, and elderly clients, and anyone living an alternative lifestyle. Every job is treated with professionalism and without judgment — unusual requests are welcome, as long as they're legal and non-sexual in nature.

Meet Betty

A sweet rescued mini Australian shepherd with a bit of separation anxiety, Betty is happy to tag along to job sites as a well-behaved shadow, anywhere dogs are welcome. Just say the word.

How Jobs Are Scoped

Before any visit, we'll talk through exactly what's needed so the right materials come along on the first trip. No formal estimate process — just a conversation and a clear hourly rate.

What's Off the Table

Licensed structural, plumbing, and electrical work isn't offered, and nothing illegal or sexual will be entertained as part of a job — that keeps things safe and professional for everyone.
